Cereal Sentinel

Save SuperMarkets More Time

Cereal Sentinel is a useful product for grocery store workers. It's inefficient for workers to consistently walk around to search for unorganized or unstocked shelves. And so, we made a product that will detect unorganized shelves or aisles that need restocking.
